Welcome to the Hub!

This isn't just an English course. It's a place where we explore big ideas, ask tough questions, and connect what we learn to the world around us.

Our First Two Adventures

Unit 1: How Music Shapes Our Lives

We'll explore how music influences our emotions, analyze trends, and learn how sound affects our focus and well-being.

Unit 2: What is the Value of Money?

We'll investigate why money exists, debate the ethics of wealth, and understand the economic forces that change prices.

The Collaboration Hub

This Course

Theory of Knowledge

To TOK: We'll ask: How do we know what a song *really* means? How does shared knowledge give money its value?

From TOK: You'll use our articles as real-world examples to analyze knowledge claims.

Literature: Fahrenheit 451

To Literature: We'll discuss how consumerism in *Fahrenheit 451* prevents critical thought.

From Literature: You'll use our language for making recommendations and discussing ethics to analyze characters.

Sustainability

To Sustainability: We'll explore the environmental impact of music streaming and "fast fashion".

From Sustainability: You'll use our language for describing trends to analyze data on carbon emissions.
